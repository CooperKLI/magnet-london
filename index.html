Q & A

Q1: How can I purchase mangnets?
A1: To purchase our mangnets, simply click the "Book Now" button under the mangnets you’re interested in. This will take you directly to the purchase page.
Q2: How long does delivery take?
A2: Delivery typically takes 3-5 days after placing your order. Since the items are small, they are usually delivered to your mailbox. However, they may also be left at the reception, delivery point, or another secure location. It's a good idea to check your mailbox after ordering. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.
Q3: Will I receive a confirmation email after placing an order?
A3: You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been dispatched or shipped, usually 1-2 days after the order is placed.
Q4: How can I make contact if I have questions?
A4: For any inquiries, customer@thekpc.com.
Q5: What is your return and exchange policy?
A5: Currently, we do not support returns or exchanges for non-quality issues.
Q6: What should I do if there’s an error with my address?
A6: Please contact us as soon as possible. We recommend double-checking your address and postal code when placing your order. Please ensure your contact information is up-to-date. Ultimately, you are be able to request "delivery options" on Royal Mail Page, and re-delivery to the correct address.
Q7: Do we accept international orders?
A7: We currently only accept orders within the UK.
Q8: Is there an additional discount for becoming a distributor?
A7: Yes, we offer distributor discounts. If you have large purchase volumes, please feel free to contact us for more details at business@thekpc.com.
