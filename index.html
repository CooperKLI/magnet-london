THE ANGEL LIGHT MAGNET
Thousands of people around the world treasure the Christmas lights, with the moment the Angel Lights illuminate the skies marking the true beginning of the festive season. In celebration of this, a special limited-edition 2024 fridge magnet has been designed, capturing the radiant beauty of the iconic Angel Lights.
The Angel Lights will shine from 9 November 2024, with displays turned on each evening on a timed schedule for a more sustainable experience. Enjoy the sparkling decorations until 5 January, with displays on Jermyn Street remaining until 2 January."
